ABOUT

Hi! My name is Julie and I am a 2nd year PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California (USC), Information Sciences Institute (ISI). I'm passionate about using machine learning methods to solve novel computational social problems. My advisor is Dr. Emilio Ferrara and I also collaborate with Dr. Kristina Lerman. Prior to USC, I obtained my B.S. from Tufts University in 2019, where I double-majored in CS and Math and conducted undergraduate research with Dr. Li-Ping Liu and Dr. Soha Hassoun.

Outside of work/school, I enjoy playing the piano and cooking :)
